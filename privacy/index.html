Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Lakeview Spine & Wellness ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at lakeviewspinewellness.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact form submissions: When you submit a message through our contact form, we collect your name, email address, phone number (optional), and message content. This information is processed through Netlify Forms.
  • Appointment booking information: When you book an appointment, you are directed to Acuity Scheduling (a third-party service). Information entered during the booking process is governed by Acuity's privacy policy.
  • Usage data: We may collect basic analytics data about how visitors use our site (pages visited, approximate location, browser type). This data is aggregated and not used to identify individuals.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • Coordinate appointment scheduling
  • Improve our website and communications

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Netlify: Website hosting and form processing (netlify.com/privacy)
  • Acuity Scheduling: Online appointment booking (acuityscheduling.com/privacy-policy)
  • Google Fonts: Typography (policies.google.com/privacy)

Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies required for site functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information submitted through our website. However, no internet transmission is completely secure. Please contact us directly if you have concerns about a specific piece of information you have submitted.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request that we delete information submitted through our contact form. To do so, email hello@lakeviewspinewellness.com with your request.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
