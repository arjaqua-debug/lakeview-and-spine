About the Clinic

A clinic built around the experience most people want but rarely get.

Founded by Dr. Elena Mercer, Lakeview Spine & Wellness was created to offer an alternative to rushed, impersonal healthcare. We believe in unhurried visits, comprehensive understanding, and a restorative environment.

Why We Exist

Why we built this practice.

We recognized a growing frustration among patients: the standard 10-minute adjustment felt inadequate. People were leaving appointments feeling unheard and under-treated.

Lakeview Spine & Wellness was designed from the ground up to be different. We stripped away the clinical coldness and replaced it with a space that feels more like a wellness retreat. More importantly, we redesigned the actual care model.

Here, appointments are longer. Treatments are layered, combining adjustments with soft tissue work and movement strategies. We take the time to understand the root cause of your discomfort, not just silence the symptom.

How We Work

Our approach to care.

We operate on a simple principle: effective care requires listening. Before we assess your spine, we want to hear your story. Understanding how your body moves through daily life is critical to creating a sustainable treatment plan.

Our methodology blends evidence-based chiropractic techniques with functional movement principles. We aren't just adjusting joints; we are educating you on how to maintain structural integrity long after you leave our studio. It's a partnership, not just a procedure.

Dr. Elena Mercer, DC

Your Provider

Dr. Elena Mercer, DC

Dr. Mercer earned her Doctorate of Chiropractic from Palmer College, driven by a deep interest in human biomechanics and holistic recovery. After practicing in high-volume clinics early in her career, she realized the standard model of care was broken.

She founded Lakeview Spine & Wellness to create an environment where quality superseded quantity. Her expertise lies in complex spinal conditions, postural correction, and supporting patients through chronic pain journeys with empathy and precision.

Outside the clinic, Dr. Mercer is an avid runner and often explores the trails around Grand Rapids, constantly studying how movement impacts overall vitality.
