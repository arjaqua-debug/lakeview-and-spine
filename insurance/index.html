Insurance & Payment

We work with most major plans — and with patients who prefer to pay directly.

Coverage for chiropractic care varies significantly by plan. If you have specific questions before your first visit, call us — we will tell you what we know and help you figure out what makes sense.

Insurance coverage

We accept a range of insurance plans including most major commercial insurance providers. Chiropractic coverage varies widely — some plans cover a set number of visits per year, others require a referral, and some do not include chiropractic at all. The best way to understand your specific coverage is to call your insurer before your first visit and ask about chiropractic benefits.

We are also happy to verify benefits on your behalf — call us at (616) 555-0192 and we will check what your plan covers before you come in.
