New Patient Forms

Complete your intake form before your first visit.

The New Patient Intake form is completed as part of the online booking process through Acuity Scheduling — you do not need to print or bring anything.

How the intake form works

Completed during online booking

When you book a New Patient Exam through our online scheduling system, the intake form appears automatically as part of the booking flow — right after you select your appointment time. You will complete it before confirming your booking.

Takes about 3–5 minutes

The form asks seven short questions about your health history, the main concern bringing you in, and consent to be contacted by the clinic. All fields are straightforward — no lengthy medical history questionnaire.

Nothing to print or bring

Your responses are received directly by our scheduling system and reviewed by Dr. Mercer before your appointment. You do not need to arrive early to fill out paperwork.
